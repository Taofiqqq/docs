## Good to know

- **Real-time rates** â€” Exchange rates update continuously. Use the [Rates](/conversion/rates) endpoint to check the current rate for any supported pair.
- **No conversion fees** â€” There are no separate fees for converting between currencies. The exchange rate you see in your quote is the all-in rate.
- **Quote validity** â€” Once you generate a quote, the rate is locked for **30 seconds**.
- **CAD is one-way only** â€” You can convert CAD to USD, but no other source currency can convert to CAD.
- **Not all payment currencies are convertible** â€” Fincra supports 17 currencies for payments, but not all are available for direct conversion. See [Supported Currencies](/payments/currencies) for the full list of payment currencies.

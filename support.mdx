## Developer support

If you're building with Fincra, most answers are in the docs. Use the search feature for quick navigation. For general questions, join the [Fincra Hub Slack Community](https://fincrahub.slack.com/join/shared_invite/zt-2qj5a4zkl-JJwlW2L~YpPNFtPBSxdM4g#/shared-invite/email) to connect with the team and other developers.

## Technical support

For integration issues not covered in the docs, reach out to our developer support team at [developers@fincra.com](mailto:developers@fincra.com).

## System status

Check real-time platform status and subscribe to incident updates at [status.fincra.com](https://status.fincra.com/).

## Overview

| | FPS | CHAPS | SWIFT |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Currency** | GBP | GBP | GBP |
| **Method** | Bank account | Bank account | Bank account |
| **Settlement** | Within 1 hour | Within 1 hour | 1-2 business days |

The United Kingdom supports three payment schemes for GBP bank account payouts.

## Payment schemes

### Faster Payments (FPS)

The UK's near-instant payment system. Best for fast, low-value payouts.

- **API value:** `fps`
- **Settlement:** Within 1 hour
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

### CHAPS

The Clearing House Automated Payment System. Best for high-value, time-sensitive payouts.

- **API value:** `chaps`
- **Settlement:** Within 1 hour
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

<Warning>
  CHAPS is only available **Monday–Friday, 08:00–17:00 UK time** (excluding UK public holidays). Payouts submitted outside this window will be processed on the next business day.
</Warning>

### SWIFT

International wire transfer network.

- **API value:** `swift`
- **Settlement:** 1-2 business days
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

## Requirements

- **Payment scheme required** — You must specify `fps`, `chaps`, or `swift` in the request.
- **Sort code** — UK bank accounts are identified by sort code + account number.
- **Sender details required** — GBP payouts require sender information for compliance.

Fincra supports payouts to 13 destinations across Africa and internationally. This page provides a complete overview of where you can send funds, which methods are available, and how fast payouts arrive.

## International payouts

Payouts in USD, GBP, and EUR via international payment schemes. You choose the scheme based on speed and cost requirements.

| Country | Currency | Schemes | Settlement |
|---|---|---|---|
| [United States](/payouts/united-states) | USD | ACH, Fedwire, SWIFT | ACH: 1-3 days, Fedwire: same day, SWIFT: 1-2 days |
| [United Kingdom](/payouts/united-kingdom) | GBP | FPS, CHAPS, SWIFT | FPS: within 1 hour, CHAPS: within 1 hour, SWIFT: 1-2 days |
| [Eurozone](/payouts/eurozone) | EUR | SEPA, SEPA Instant, SWIFT | SEPA: within 1 day, SEPA Instant: seconds, SWIFT: 1-2 days |

<Note>
  USD payouts via SWIFT extend beyond the United States — you can send USD to recipients in many countries globally where banks accept USD wire transfers. See [United States](/payouts/united-states) for details.
</Note>

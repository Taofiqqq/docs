## Overview

| | Bank Account |
|---|---|
| **Currency** | ZAR |
| **Scheme** | Local rails |
| **Settlement** | Instant |

South Africa supports payouts to bank accounts only. Mobile money payouts are not available.

## Bank account payouts

Send ZAR to South African bank accounts via local clearing rails.

- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`
- **No payment scheme required** — Fincra routes automatically.
- **Bank SWIFT code required** — South Africa uses `bankSwiftCode` for bank identification.

<Note>
  Mobile money payouts to South Africa are not supported at this time. Only bank account is available.
</Note>

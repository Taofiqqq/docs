## Overview

| | SEPA | SEPA Instant | SWIFT |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Currency** | EUR | EUR | EUR |
| **Method** | Bank account | Bank account | Bank account |
| **Settlement** | Within 1 day | Within seconds | 1-2 business days |

The Eurozone supports three payment schemes for EUR bank account payouts.

## Payment schemes

### SEPA

The Single Euro Payments Area network. Covers all EU/EEA countries. Best for routine EUR payouts.

- **API value:** `sepa`
- **Settlement:** Within 1 day
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

### SEPA Instant

Real-time EUR payments across the SEPA network. Best for urgent payouts.

- **API value:** `sepa_instant`
- **Settlement:** Within seconds
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

### SWIFT

International wire transfer network.

- **API value:** `swift`
- **Settlement:** 1-2 business days
- **Payment destination:** `bank_account`

## Requirements

- **Payment scheme required** — You must specify `sepa`, `sepa_instant`, or `swift` in the request.
- **IBAN required** — Eurozone bank accounts are identified by IBAN.
- **Sender details required** — EUR payouts require sender information for compliance.

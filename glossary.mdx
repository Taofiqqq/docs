### Bank Verification Number (BVN)

A unique 11-digit identifier assigned to individuals by the Central Bank of Nigeria to link all their bank accounts. Fincra's BVN resolution API allows you to verify a customer's identity using their BVN. See /docs/bvn-resolution-1.

### PCI DSS Compliance

The Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is a set of security requirements for organizations that handle payment card data. Fincra is PCI DSS compliant, meaning cardholder data processed through our platform is secured to industry standards.

### Webhooks

Real-time HTTP notifications sent from Fincra to your server when events occur on your account, such as completed payouts, successful collections, or failed transactions. See /webhooks/overview.

### NIP

NIBSS Instant Payments

### RTGS

Real-Time Gross Settlement
